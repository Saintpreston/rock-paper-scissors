A common experience that many of us share is playing the iconic game Rock, Paper, Scissors. I never realized playing rock paper scissors with a friend to determine who had to do a small or unfavorable task, could be classified as Divination.


As an ode to the game that played a pivotal role in many of our childhoods. I invite you to play against these rock paper scissors systems I made.

  • This is one impossible to beat.
  • This one is impossible to lose against,
  • And lastly, this one is fair.
